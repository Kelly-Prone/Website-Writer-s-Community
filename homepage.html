What is the "Writer's community"?

Explore your imagination...

Here you can write literary ideas. Use your imagination to write what is most comfortable and what makes you the happiest. You will have a community eagerly waiting to read the news.
Explore .... Share! In the "Writer's Community" you can organize your ideas and adapt them as needed. We ask that you list these ideas so as not to get lost, just click on the "organize" tab.

But where do I start? Here's the best part! In the "Write" tab, you will have a tool developed for you to write your inspirations and then (if you want) to share with your friends. Share a snippet of your idea in our "community", search for experiences, opinions, corrections, new ideas and more!

We are a growing platform and we want thousands of people to enjoy and develop new books! Reading, strengthens our imagination and creation and helps thousands of people in the most diverse problems. Let's make a better place together here! And for that to happen, leave your feedback in the "Contact" tab.

Easy and practical! Join our community now =)
